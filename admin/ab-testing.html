Subject Line Best Practices

1
Personalise with [Name]
Subject lines with the recipient's first name or company name see 18-26% higher open rates. Always use merge tags.
2
Lead with Curiosity
Questions and open loops outperform statements. "Quick question about..." consistently beats "We can help you with..."
3
Keep Under 50 Characters
Short subject lines display fully on mobile. Aim for 6-10 words. Truncated subjects lose up to 15% of opens.
4
Avoid Spam Triggers
Words like "FREE", "ACT NOW", all caps, and excessive punctuation trip spam filters. Keep it natural and conversational.
5
Use Specificity
Concrete numbers and details build trust. "I found 3 issues..." beats "We noticed some problems..." every time.
6
Test One Variable at a Time
Change only the subject line between variants. If you alter body copy too, you cannot attribute the difference to the subject.
