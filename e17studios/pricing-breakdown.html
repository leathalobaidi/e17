Common Questions, Honest Answers

We hear these all the time. Here is why they should not hold you back.

📱

"I already have a Facebook page."

A Facebook page is rented space — Meta controls who sees your posts (often less than 5% of followers). A website is your property. It shows up on Google, works 24/7, and builds credibility that social media alone cannot match.

🛠

"Why not build it myself on Wix?"

You could — if you have 40+ hours spare for design, copywriting, SEO, mobile testing, and ongoing maintenance. Most owners try, get stuck, and end up with a half-finished site that hurts more than it helps. We handle everything so you can run your business.

🏆

"My competitor doesn't have one either."

Exactly — that is your first-mover advantage. When customers search Google for your service, you will be the only result with a professional site. By the time competitors catch on, you will already own the top spot.

💰

"I can't afford it."

Consider the cost of not having a website. The average local business loses an estimated £18/day in missed revenue from customers who search online and go to a competitor instead. That is £6,570/year — versus £348/year for our Starter plan.

⏰

"I'm too busy to deal with it."

That is the whole point. We handle everything — design, content, updates, hosting, SEO. You spend zero time on your website. Need a change? Send us a message and it is done, usually within hours.

🔓

"What if I want to cancel?"

No contracts. Cancel anytime. Seriously. We do not lock you in because we believe you will stay for the results, not because of small print. Plus, you are covered by our 14-day money-back guarantee.

Pricing FAQ

Got questions about our pricing? Here are the answers.

What is included in the setup?
Everything. We design your website, write the copy, optimise images, set up SEO, configure your domain, install an SSL certificate, and get you live. There is no setup fee — it is all included in your monthly plan. Most sites are live within 48 hours.
Are there any hidden fees?
Absolutely not. Your monthly price covers everything: hosting, SSL, design, content updates, support, and SEO. The only additional cost would be your domain name if you do not already own one (typically around £10–£15/year), and we will help you set that up.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?
Yes, you can change your plan at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately and we will pro-rate the cost for the current billing period. Downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing cycle. No penalties, no paperwork.
Do I own the website?
You own all the content — text, images, branding — that is always yours. The website design and hosting are part of the service. If you cancel, we can export your content for you. If you are on the Premium plan for 12+ months, we offer a full handover option including source files.
What happens if I cancel?
You can cancel any time with no penalties, fees, or awkward conversations. Your site remains active until the end of your current billing period. We will export any content you need and help with the transition. We also offer a 14-day money-back guarantee for new customers.
Do you offer discounts for multiple locations?
Yes. If you have more than one business location, we offer bundle pricing. Each additional site gets a 20% discount off the standard plan rate. Contact us for a custom quote — we will create a package that works for your entire operation.
