Yes. We submit your website to Google Search Console and optimise it with Schema.org markup, meta tags, and local SEO best practices. Most sites start appearing in search results within 2-4 weeks. Rankings improve over time as Google recognises your site's authority.

Schema markup is structured data that helps Google understand your business ‚Äî your name, address, opening hours, reviews, and services. It can make your Google listing more prominent with rich snippets (star ratings, opening hours) that attract more clicks.
